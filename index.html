Computer Science

By pure chance, I stumbled into a Computer Science class in high school and ended up falling in love with programming. Thanks to my teacher's recommendation, I applied to the University of Waterloo for Computer Science and it turned out to be one of the best decisions I ever made. Waterloo was a wild ride, but I loved every moment of it. I made amazing friends, took some really cool courses, and had the chance to try out six different internships.

In addition to coding, I have a wide range of interests that keep me busy. I’ve always been super into movies and shows (I have obsessively rated over a thousand of them on IMDB). I also love reading (you can check out my Goodreads here). I love traveling and will take any chance I get to explore a new place. I’ve recently started learning piano (a lifelong dream) and I’ve been really enjoying it! I’m also super competitive in any and all games. Interests

I thrive on pushing my boundaries and taking on new challenges. Nothing excites me more than delving into unfamiliar technologies and languages, and pushing myself to master them. My passion lies in product work, with a focus on frontend and fullstack development. I take pride in creating interfaces that are not only high-performing and visually stunning, but also accessible and user-friendly. Collaborating with designers is one of my favorite things to do, as I love learning about what users truly want and need.
