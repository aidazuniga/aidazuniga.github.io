Instructions
  • First select the number of discs you wish to play with (2-9) and press "Play".
  • The goal of the game is to rebuild the original post in a different post.
  • You can only move the disc that is at the top.
  • You cannot put a a disc on top of one that is smaller than it.
  • In order to move the discs, simply click on it.
  • Once a disc has been selected, either:
    • Click on the post number you wish to put it on.
    • Or press the numbers 1, 2 or 3 on your keyboard to select post.
